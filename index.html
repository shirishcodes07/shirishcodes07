Your Questions Answered

Q: How do I enroll my child in your school?

A: To enroll, visit school and fill out the application form and submit the required documents. Our admissions team will guide you through the process.

Q: Are scholarships or financial aid available?

A: Yes, scholarships and financial assistance are available based on academic performance and financial need. Please contact the school office for details.

Q: Do you have sports and extracurricular activities?

A: Yes, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities including sports, music, arts, and clubs to encourage all-round development.
